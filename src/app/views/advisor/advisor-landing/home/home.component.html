Legacy Life Plans- shifting advisors from being transactional sellers into relationship builders.

Legacy Life Plans is an encrypted, secure, digital safe deposit box where your clients can organize, file, and store their important life documents and information. In the event of a debilitating illness or death, your client’s loved ones will have predetermined managed access and a roadmap to handle your client’s affairs or final wishes.

Relieve the stress of heirs, estate executors, and administrators by using this tool to provide a step-by-step blueprint, which your clients can follow to organize end-of-life directives.
