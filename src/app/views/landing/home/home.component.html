Legacy Life Plans- Keep you in control when life makes unexpected turns.

Legacy Life Plans is an encrypted, secure, digital safe deposit box. Here, you can organize, file, and store important life documents and information. In the event of a debilitating illness or death, your loved ones will have predetermined managed access and a roadmap to handle your affairs and/or final wishes. Relieve the stress of heirs, estate executors, and administrators. Use this tool to provide a step-by-step blueprint, that your family can follow to handle your directives.

Legacy Life Plans Process

Living well starts with a plan and ends as a legacy. LEGACY LIFE PLANS is an event driven tool. Every new policy, account, deed, subscription or snapshot of a life event CAN be added to your LEGACY LIFE PLAN account. Any new family event calls for updating your LEGACY LIFE PLAN and many of its contents

Organize, file, record, and share your memories and aspirations with the people you love. Make the end of your life easier on those you leave behind. Give heirs and executors safe, yet convenient, access to your:
